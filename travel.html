Sierra Nevada Brewing, also in Chico, rescheduled Oktoberfest to our wedding weekend. This means hotels will book out soon, especially after their tickets go on sale on Aug 12. If our recommended hotels are booked, we've also provided some alternative hotels that are nearby.

Flights

Sacramento International Airport (SMF) is the closest airport to Chico - it's about an hour and a half drive via rental car.

San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is also an option, but it's a further drive than Sacramento. If you take this route, we recommend stopping at the world-famous Granzella's Restaurant on I-5, where you can enjoy a nice meal in the company of some serious taxidermy and an M16-shaped guitar signed by Ted Nugent.
